Technical and Vocational Education and Training Authority

The Technical and Vocational Education and Training Authority (TVETA) is a public corporate agency established under the Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) Act No. 29 of 2013 to regulate and coordinate training in the country through licensing, registration and accreditation of programs, institutions and trainers.

Partnership Success In creating Job Hubs.

Konza Technopolis, TVETA, Africa Center for Advanced Technology (ACAT) and Lish AI Labs partnered to create a 100 seater Job Hub at Kabete National Polytechnic on 6th December 2022 where his Excellency the President was the guest of honor to launch the first lab. Konza Technopolis provided and installed the computers, TVETA provided their Kabete Institution, ACAT jobs sourcing while Lish AI Labs provided contracted jobs training and lab management.His Excellency the President challenged the PS for ICT & Digital Economy, Eng. Tanui to create 1 million jobs in less than 5 years. It was also resolved that this partnership will roll-out labs across the country's TVETA Institutions in the fastest time possible under Kenya Youth Empowerment Program (KYEP).

Mr. David Waweru explains to the President!

Lish AI Labs cloud worker, David Waweru presents his 14 months online job experience at Lish and how it has transformed his life by creating a second income stream that was funded from his earnings. He also explained that 14 months ago before joining Lish for online jobs, he worked ata a local supermarket with a monthly salary of 9,000/= but at Lish, he could make a minimum of 10,000/= a week.
