Partnership Success In creating Job Hubs.

Konza Technopolis, TVETA, Africa Center for Advanced Technology (ACAT) and Lish AI Labs partnered to create a 100 seater Job Hub at Kabete National Polytechnic on 6th December 2022 where his Excellency the President was the guest of honor to launch the first lab. Konza Technopolis provided and installed the computers, TVETA provided their Kabete Institution, ACAT jobs sourcing while Lish AI Labs provided contracted jobs training and lab management.His Excellency the President challenged the PS for ICT & Digital Economy, Eng. Tanui to create 1 million jobs in less than 5 years. It was also resolved that this partnership will roll-out labs across the country's TVETA Institutions in the fastest time possible under Kenya Youth Empowerment Program (KYEP).
